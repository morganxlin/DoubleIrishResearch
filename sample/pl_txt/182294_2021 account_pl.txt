Principal activity
Gartner Ireland Limited (the "Company") is a subsidiary of Gartner, Inc. The Company is engaged in the provision and
distribution of research products and advisory services. Additionally, the Company licenses intellectual properties.
Together with Gartner, Inc., and other group companies, the Company delivers actionable, objective insight to executives
and their teams. Gartner's expert guidance and tools enable faster, smarter decisions and stronger performance on an
organisation's mission critical priorities.
