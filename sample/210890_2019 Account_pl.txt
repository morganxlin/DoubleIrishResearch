MERIT MEDICAL IRELAND LIMITED
DIRECTORS'REPORT
The directors present their annual report on the affairs of the company, together with the financial statements and
auditor's report for the financial year ended 31 December: 2019.
BUSINESS REVIEW
The company continues to be a contract manufacturer for its parent company, incorporating the Basixs, Custom Kits,
Wire and Hemostatis product lines. The company continues to manufacture, supply and sell its proprietary guidewire
product range in its own name. The directors are satisfied with the company's progress to date and its state of affairs at
the balance sheet date.
RESULTS AND DIVIDENDS
The results for the financial year and state of affairs of the company are set out in the Statement of Comprehensive
Income and the Balance Sheet on pages 10 and 11 respectively.
FUTURE DEVELOPMENTS
The company intends to continue to research, develop, produce, market and distribute medical devices for the treatment
of cardiovascular disorders worldwide. Merit Medical Systems Inc. continues to seek strategic investment opportunities
for its wider group.
