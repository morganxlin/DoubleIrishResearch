Principal activities, review of the business and future developments
Janssen Pharmaceutical Unlimited Company is an investment holding company. It is a fully owned subsidiary of
Johnson & Johnson.
The company has received dividend income from its subsidiaries Depuy Ireland Unlimited Company in amount of
€1,400,000,000, Janssen Pharmaceutical Sciences Unlimited Company in the amount of €100,000,000 and
Janssen Alzheimer Immunotherapy (Holdings) Ltd in the amount of €11,187,389 (2019: Johnson & Johnson
European Treasury Company €1,204,721).
The principal activity of the company is to act as an investment holding company now and in future financial years.
As of the financial statement issuance date, the country, region and world are continuing to be impacted by the
coronavirus (COVID-19). Although the situation is still evolving at the date of issue of these financial statements,
there is no tangible impact on the Company's activity however, the impact of this situation on the Company in the
future cannot be predicted. Management will continue to closely monitor the potential impact of these events and
will take all possible measures to mitigate possible consequence.
The company's financial year ends on the Sunday closest to 31 December, being 3 January 2021 for the current
year (53 weeks) and 29 December 2019 for the prior year (52 weeks).
Profits, dividends and reserves
The profit for the financial year, after taxation, amounted to €2,324,582,000 (2019: loss of €538,276,000).
The directors recommended the dividend payment of €15,976,612,000  (2019: €Nil).
The company's statement of financial position shows total equity at 3 January 2021 of €8,330,793,000 (2019:
€21,982,823,000).
